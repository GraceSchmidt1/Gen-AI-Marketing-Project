# ICP Segment Reference
Source: Q1 ICP Segments and Content Pillar Reference Guide (02/08/2026, Grace Schmidt)

---

## Oncology Operations Leader

**Roles:** Practice administrators, executive directors, COOs, directors of operations, clinic administrators, regional operations directors

**Buyer role:** Economic buyer or co-buyer; involves finance and IT in final approval

**Primary motivations:**
- Operational reliability and minimizing firefighting
- Reducing patient delays
- Clear, trustworthy dashboards

**Content interests:**
- Reducing time to treatment
- Stabilizing staffing
- Lowering operational rework
- Improving cycle time from order to infusion
- Standardizing workflows across sites
- Measurable ROI and vendor accountability

**Associated content pillars:**
- Thought Leadership & Industry Insights
- Events & Partnerships
- Patient-Centered Storytelling

---

## Oncology Financial Leader

**Roles:** Revenue cycle managers, billing managers, directors of patient financial services, finance directors, controllers, CFOs, revenue integrity managers, revenue cycle leaders

**Buyer role:** Often controls budget; requires IT sign-off

**Primary motivations:**
- Hard numbers, clear financial outcomes
- Before vs. after metrics
- Strong risk controls (audit logs, traceability, compliance posture)

**Content interests:**
- Denial reduction
- First pass approval rates
- Days in accounts receivable
- Clean claims and appeal win rates
- Leakage detection
- Payer contract performance
- Predictable cash flow
- Audit defensibility

**Associated content pillars:**
- Thought Leadership & Industry Insights
- Product Development & Innovations
- Patient-Centered Storytelling

---

## Oncology Technical Leader

**Roles:** IT directors, systems administrators, CIOs, CISOs, security officers, clinical informaticists

**Buyer role:** Required sign-off; skeptical of fragile integrations

**Primary motivations:**
- System reliability
- Skeptical of screen scraping or manual mapping solutions
- Need clear deployment models and escalation paths

**Content interests:**
- Integration simplicity, SSO, RBAC
- Audit trails and uptime
- Minimal workflow disruption
- FHIR and HL7 interoperability
- Data governance
- SOC 2 and HIPAA compliance evidence
- Clear product roadmaps

**Associated content pillars:**
- Thought Leadership & Industry Insights
- Product Development & Innovations
- Events & Partnerships

---

## Oncology Clinic Leader

**Roles:** Medical directors, physician partners, lead oncologists, nursing directors, infusion nurse managers, APP leads, clinical operations directors, chief clinical officers

**Buyer role:** Influencer; trust and explainability are critical

**Primary motivations:**
- No tolerance for tools that add clicks or interfere with clinical judgment
- Motivated by clinical sanity and patient impact

**Content interests:**
- Keeping patients on schedule
- Reducing administrative burden on clinical staff
- Minimizing peer-to-peer calls and retroactive documentation
- Maintaining guideline consistency
- Reducing treatment delays from authorization issues
- Clarity on documentation requirements

**Associated content pillars:**
- Thought Leadership & Industry Insights
- Patient-Centered Storytelling
- Team Spotlights & Company Culture
