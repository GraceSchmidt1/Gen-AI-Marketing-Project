# Workflow & Tools SOP

*Source: Social_Media_Marketing_SOP_Training.docx — Consultant: Sydney Ford*

---

## Workspaces & Roles

### Microsoft Planner Boards
- **"Marketing" board:** Projects, campaigns, training, analytics, templates, brand assets. Keep files centralized on the Files tab — not in chat or scattered links.
- **"Content" board:** Individual social posts and newsletters moving through the workflow.

### Scheduling Tool
- **Loomly:** Post creation, per-channel edits, status tracking, and analytics snapshots.

### Cadence
- Weekly board reviews
- Daily sync between Planner and Loomly to keep statuses aligned
- Archive monthly

---

## Step-by-Step Workflow

### 1. Idea Intake & Triage
- Add ideas to the **Content Planner Board → Ideas bucket**
- Include: who, what, when, where, why, which asset, any visuals or links — everything inside the task (no email threads)
- Label the idea by **content pillar** and **channel**
- Reviewer checks Ideas on schedule → moves approved items to **To Do**, assigns owner, sets due date

### 2. Plan & Prepare in Planner
- Convert approved idea to **To Do** with owner and due date (both mandatory)
- Attach assets to the Planner task: copy drafts, images, video, links to Loomly items
- Move to **In Progress** when drafting begins

### 3. Create Post in Loomly
- Create post, select channels, drop in caption, links, and assets
- **Per-channel optimization:**
  - Adjust copy and timing per platform
  - Ensure correct image/video dimensions
  - Choose or upload video cover image when needed
  - Tag people/orgs on LinkedIn via @mention in Loomly (tag manually after publish if tag doesn't resolve due to privacy)
  - Keep Loomly URL shortener on unless there's a reason to show the raw URL
  - Default posting time: **10 AM** unless data suggests otherwise
- Set status to **Pending Approval** when ready for review
- Reviewer approves → Loomly moves to **Scheduled**
- If changes requested → remains a draft, use comments to list edits needed

### 4. Keep Planner & Loomly in Sync
- While drafting: Planner task stays **In Progress**
- When Loomly is **Scheduled**: mark Planner task **Complete**
- If edits requested: keep Planner task **In Progress** until Loomly is Approved & Scheduled

### 5. Publishing Hygiene
- Check link previews; re-add if Loomly drops them
- Confirm tags resolve and cover images look clean in feeds
- Verify image/reel dimensions per platform at the fine-tune step before scheduling

### 6. Post-Publish Analytics
- **Monthly analytics SOP:** Use Loomly analytics to capture link clicks and post performance
- **LinkedIn metrics to track:** Impressions, engagements, clicks, follower growth
- **Instagram metrics to track:** Reach, engagement rate, saves, shares, follower growth
- Update the monthly dashboard and note lessons learned
- Plan a follow-up analytics review with Grace

### 7. Maintenance Routines
- **Weekly:** Review both Marketing and Content boards, align statuses with Loomly, clear stale tasks
- **Monthly:** Archive completed tasks, deliver roll-up analytics report with recommendations
