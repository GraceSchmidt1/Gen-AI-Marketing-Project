# Hidalga Brand & Values Reference

## Mission Statement
Improve healthcare systems through innovative technology solutions that streamline administrative processes, enhance provider efficiency, and improve patient outcomes. Empower healthcare professionals with intuitive software to optimize workflow efficiency, increase revenue, and drive positive change in the industry — ultimately improving patient care worldwide.

## Vision Statement
A future where seeking disease treatment no longer instills fear. Improve and extend lives through innovative healthcare technology. Provide equitable access to quality care for all, ensuring everyone benefits from modern advancements. Positively impact patients, families, and healthcare providers with tools that streamline administrative processes and manage treatment progress and resources to conquer chronic disease.

## Core Values

1. **Innovation** — Embrace change through creative technological solutions. Stay curious, explore trends, spark fresh ideas, and continuously improve.
2. **Efficiency** — Streamline processes so healthcare providers can focus on what matters. Optimize workflows, enhance revenue, and empower professionals with user-friendly software.
3. **Integrity** — Honesty when challenges arise. Speak up, ask for resources, and follow through with transparency and ethics.
4. **Accountability** — Lead with transparency, make thoughtful decisions together, set clear goals, and hold each other to high standards.
5. **Collaboration** — Communicate openly and kindly. Value teamwork, celebrate collaboration, ensure everyone feels heard.
6. **Mission Alignment** — Ground all actions in the mission. Regularly revisit purpose, align strategies to drive positive change in healthcare.
7. **Clinic-Centric Focus** — Patient, family, and provider well-being at the center. Strive for equitable access to high-quality healthcare for all.
8. **Work Culture** — Inclusive environment where every voice is valued. Share a positive vision, encourage teamwork, celebrate milestones. Merit is rewarded equally.

## Brand Tone Standards

### Voice: Direct. Confident. Evidence-based. Empathetic.

Content should:
- Lead with a clearly defined problem rather than a general idea
- Connect industry commentary to how it affects clinic workflows or financial outcomes
- Make it immediately obvious that content is relevant to the reader's day-to-day responsibilities
- Tie team updates and announcements back to expertise, product development, or mission alignment

### Forbidden Words & Phrases
Never use the following under any circumstances:
- "seamless"
- "transform" / "transformative"
- "smarter"
- "revolutionize"
- "eliminate"
- Em dashes (—) in copy
- Hype-heavy language
- Vague jargon
- Exaggerated claims

### Underperforming Content Patterns to Avoid
- Posts that remain too general or lack a clear problem statement
- Content that doesn't signal a specific audience in the opening lines
- Team/culture posts not tied back to expertise or product relevance
- High-level or abstract messaging without operational specificity

### Strong Content Patterns
- Specific, tied to financial or operational pain points
- Direct messaging with a clearly stated problem early
- Industry commentary connected to clinic workflows or financial outcomes
- Prior authorization challenges, denial-related revenue loss, treatment delays, administrative inefficiency — these topics consistently drive stronger engagement
