# ICP Segments & Content Pillar Reference

*Last updated: 02/08/2026 — Grace Schmidt*

---

## ICP Segments

### 1. Oncology Operations Leader
**Roles:** Practice administrators, executive directors, COOs, directors of operations, clinic administrators, regional operations directors.

**Mindset:** Focused on operational reliability, minimizing firefighting, reducing patient delays, maintaining clear dashboards. Often the economic buyer or co-buyer; involves finance and IT in final approvals.

**Key interests:** Reducing time to treatment, stabilizing staffing, lowering operational rework, improving cycle time from order to infusion, standardizing workflows across sites, measurable ROI, vendor accountability.

**Content pillars:**
- Thought Leadership & Industry Insights
- Events & Partnerships
- Patient-Centered Storytelling

---

### 2. Oncology Financial Leader
**Roles:** Revenue cycle managers, billing managers, directors of patient financial services, finance directors, controllers, CFOs, revenue integrity managers, revenue cycle leaders.

**Mindset:** Driven by hard numbers — baselines, deltas, payback periods. Responds to before/after metrics and strong risk controls (audit logs, traceability, compliance). Controls budget; requires IT sign-off.

**Key interests:** Denial reduction, first-pass approval rates, days in AR, clean claims, appeal win rates, leakage detection, payer contract performance, predictable cash flow, audit defensibility.

**Content pillars:**
- Thought Leadership & Industry Insights
- Product Development & Innovations
- Patient-Centered Storytelling

---

### 3. Oncology Technical Leader
**Roles:** IT directors, systems administrators, CIOs, CISOs, security officers, clinical informaticists.

**Mindset:** Concerned with system reliability and off-hours failure. Skeptical of fragile screen scraping or constant manual mapping. Needs clear deployment models, escalation paths, and implementation plans that don't overwhelm teams.

**Key interests:** Integration simplicity, SSO, RBAC, audit trails, uptime, minimal workflow disruption, manageable support burden, FHIR/HL7 interoperability, data governance, SOC 2/HIPAA compliance, clear product roadmaps.

**Content pillars:**
- Thought Leadership & Industry Insights
- Product Development & Innovations
- Events & Partnerships

---

### 4. Oncology Clinic Leader
**Roles:** Medical directors, physician partners, lead oncologists, nursing directors, infusion nurse managers, APP leads, clinical operations directors, chief clinical officers.

**Mindset:** No tolerance for tools that add clicks or interfere with clinical judgment. Motivated by clinical sanity and patient impact. Trust is critical — explainability, accurate data extraction, ability to override outputs.

**Key interests:** Keeping patients on schedule, reducing administrative burden on clinical staff, minimizing peer-to-peer calls and retroactive documentation, guideline consistency, reducing treatment delays from auth issues, clarity on documentation requirements.

**Content pillars:**
- Thought Leadership & Industry Insights
- Patient-Centered Storytelling
- Team Spotlights & Company Culture

## Content Pillars

### Thought Leadership & Industry Insights
**Goal:** Position Hidalga as a leader in healthcare innovation by sharing industry trends, research, and insights.
**Content types:** Case studies, beta test outcomes, data-driven insights on healthcare inefficiencies, expert interviews.
**Platforms:** LinkedIn (primary).

### Product Development & Innovations
**Goal:** Highlight Hidalga's cutting-edge technology and ongoing product development.
**Content types:** Product updates, new feature launches, behind-the-scenes development updates, anticipated outcomes from tests.
**Platforms:** LinkedIn (primary).

### Events & Partnerships
**Goal:** Showcase participation in healthcare competitions, events, and strategic partnerships.
**Content types:** Live event updates, partnership announcements with clinics, real-time conference posts, competition highlights.
**Platforms:** All channels.

### Patient-Centered Storytelling
**Goal:** Humanize Hidalga's mission and emphasize the impact of healthcare improvements.
**Content types:** Patient journey stories, before-and-after scenarios, personal narratives about healthcare delays and solutions.
**Platforms:** Instagram and Facebook (primary), less on LinkedIn.

### Team Spotlights & Company Culture
**Goal:** Build authenticity and trust with the audience by highlighting Hidalga's team and culture.
**Content types:** Team member spotlights, behind-the-scenes content, motivational quotes, employee-driven stories.
**Platforms:** All channels.

## Tagging Protocol
Tag every item in Loomly and Planner with:
- The appropriate **ICP segment**
- The appropriate **content pillar**

This connects performance data back to specific audience segments and enables ongoing evaluation of what is resonating with each group.
