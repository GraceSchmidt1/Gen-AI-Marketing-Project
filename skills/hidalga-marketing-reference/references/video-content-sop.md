# Video Content SOP

*Source: Q1_Biweekly_video_engine.docx — Hidalga Technologies*

---

## Purpose
Standardize how Hidalga plans, prompts, records, and lightly edits short-form video content for social media and related marketing use. Ensure consistent, evidence-based video creation aligned with marketing strategy, content pillars, approval standards, and workflow systems.

---

## Scope
This SOP applies to:
- 30–60 second short-form videos
- Canva-generated videos
- Team-recorded videos for LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, X, and conference/event use
- Videos created for: thought leadership, product awareness, patient-centered messaging, partnerships, team visibility

Use alongside: Marketing Strategy, Loomly SOP, and Planner SOP.

---

## Core Video Standards

All Hidalga video content must be:
- **Direct**
- **Confident**
- **Evidence-based**
- **Empathetic**
- **Grounded in:** oncology operations, prior authorization workflow realities, and revenue cycle pain points

### Do NOT use:
- Hype-heavy language
- Vague jargon
- Exaggerated claims
- Em dashes
- The words: "seamless," "transform," "smarter," "revolutionize," or "eliminate"

---

## Video Structure

Strong video content:
1. **Opens with a clear, relevant problem** — immediately relevant to the target viewer
2. **Avoids unnecessary complexity** — keep it direct and specific
3. **Delivers a concise takeaway** — make it immediately useful for busy operators
4. Ends with a CTA appropriate for the platform

---

## Effective Video Formats

- 20–40 second explanations of prior authorization challenges
- Breakdowns of industry updates tied to clinic impact
- Walkthroughs of workflow inefficiencies or product functionality

---

## AI-Assisted Script Generation

To avoid re-prompting repeatedly, maintain a dedicated **ChatGPT project** for Hidalga video content generation.

### What to include in the project:
- Hidalga Marketing Strategy
- Loomly SOP
- Planner SOP
- Audience targets (ICP segments)
- Content pillars
- Brand tone rules
- Core value proposition
- Video structure requirements
- CTA options

### Project purpose:
Serve as standing context for video creation so prompts remain focused and efficient. Use it to generate Canva-ready video prompts and script drafts without reloading context each time.

---

## Cadence
- Minimum **one video every two weeks**
- Prioritize for **LinkedIn first**, then adapt for other platforms
- Track in Planner and schedule via Loomly per the standard workflow
